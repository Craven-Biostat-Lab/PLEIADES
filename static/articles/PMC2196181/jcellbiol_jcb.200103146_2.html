Figure S1. Quiescent mouse embryo fibroblasts, microinjected with an expression vector for GFP-Eps8 (648–821) were kept at 37°C in a CO2 incubator mounted on an inverted microscope (Olympus). Images were captured every 15 min with a coded CCD camera (Hamamatsu). GFP epifluorescence was detected with an FITC filter set (Chromo Technology). A set of six consecutive images captured at the indicated time is also shown. Arrows point to ruffles. Arrowheads point to spreading lamellipodia where the GFP-effector domain is relocalized. Bar, 10 mm.
